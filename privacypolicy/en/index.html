Privacy Policy

Otto App (iOS and Android)

1. Collected Data

Otto only collects the following information provided by the user during registration:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Password

* Otto does not collect location, images, audio, device information, or track browsing activity.

2. Purpose of Data Use

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Create and manage the user account;
  • Allow login and authentication in the app;
  • Send communications related to app functionality (e.g., account or subscription notifications).

3. Storage and Security

  • Data is stored in a secure environment with reasonable measures to prevent unauthorized access.
  • The user is responsible for keeping their password confidential.

4. Data Sharing

  • Otto does not share, sell, or rent personal data to third parties.
  • Payment information is neither accessed nor stored by Otto, as subscriptions are processed directly by Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

5. Subscriptions and Free Trial

  • Users are entitled to a 2-month free trial after registration.
  • After this period, a paid in-app subscription is required to continue using the app.
  • Billing and cancellations are handled directly by Apple and Google stores.

6. User Rights

According to applicable law (including the Brazilian General Data Protection Law – LGPD), users have the right to:

  • Access the data they provided;
  • Correct incomplete or outdated data;
  • Request deletion of their account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, contact us via email: otto.fliper.studios@gmail.com.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. In case of significant changes, users will be notified via the app or registered email.
